Skill Embassy

Skill Embassy is a product school that prepares entry level designers, developers and product mangagers transition into the tech industry by working across a cross-functional team.

Project Overview

Skill Embassy helps entry level product designers, entry level product managers, entry level software developers solve teamwork and communication skillsets by facilitating cross-descipline product teams in going from ideation to launch.

Some of the roles I played:

  • Put students profitability first, whether they are independent or group
  • Invest in the future: work for bigger, long-term deals
  • Find partners who are excited about Skill Embassy and share similar values
  • Save and share with the team to make sure we're making things as useful as possible
  • Provide any and all applicable visual, resources and links - including answers to questions studends have not thought about.
